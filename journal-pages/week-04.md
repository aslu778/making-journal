## Local AI with Ollama

![alt text](<../assets/week-04/Screenshot 2026-04-01 213100.png>)

using Ollama was very interesting, at first when I gave it a prompt it begins typing out as if a person is actually responding to me. It feels more real of a response compared to chatGPT and it lets me know what I am missing in the prompt which may be required and then decides to cover different areas or topics so that it might include what I needed. As I continued to wait for the prompt to end, its like it keeps asking its own prompt and keeps going on and on which is good as it becomes very detailed. But the negative thing about using Ollama is that it causes a lot of lag on my laptop, it writes prompts at the speed of someone typing or slower than that. Overall i think the quality of answers given is very thorough and deep compared to other genAI but it still had its cons.
