# Independent Live Data Visualisation



## Reflection
I think that this protocol is clear enough for someone to follow and produce meaningful results like how I did as behaviours of cats are distinct, and maps to a simple physical action. My artefact changes and accumulates overtime as its very unpredictable by depending on the directions of my cat’s behaviour, creating a layered, branching, sometimes chaotic structure. The final reveal of my physical data reveals:
- The tactile rhythm of calm vs. chaotic moments
- The spatial density of certain behaviours
- The embodied presence of the cat’s personality
- The material weight of active vs. inactive periods
It became a sculptural portrait of my cat’s behaviour instead of just a flat screen presentation of the data. 

This weeks learning has been really challenging, its been hard to get my head wraped around using comand line and how the live data works and getting the weather visualisations but im glad at the end I managed to get it all done.
